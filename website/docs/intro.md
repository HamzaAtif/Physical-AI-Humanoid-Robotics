## Learning Objectives

By the end of this course, you will be able to:

- Understand the principles of Physical AI and embodied intelligence

- Computer Science and Engineering students
- Robotics researchers and practitioners
- AI/ML engineers interested in physical systems
- Anyone interested in the intersection of AI and robotics
